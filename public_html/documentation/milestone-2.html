Summary

The site will consist of six sections in one long page with anchor links around the page, a downloadable image file, a native contact form, and a link that opens in the user's email with a populated subject line "Request Estimate."

Mobile Version

The mobile version will be as close to the desktop version as possible, with a hamburger menu taking the place of the clickable links of the man menu. The layout will be more narrow and the small fonts that appear on the desktop may be relocated to areas where they can be enlarged and more legible.

1. Menu Bar/Hamburger Menu

The menu/hamburger will be located on the top of the page in the far right corner. There will be three options that include: ABOUT WORK and CONTACT. The hamburger menu will drop down with a translucent background. The anchor links will resolve farther down the page at each named section.

2. Landing Page

The landing page will have a title "Web Developer & Consultant, Full-stack." There will be an image of me below that on the right with my name below it. There will be a list of my specialities and highlight my years of experience. I will also mention the populations I prioritize. There will be a big red button inviting visitors to request an estimate.

3. About Section

This section lists the experience that qualifies me to develop and consult for organizations, including my positions, my technology proficiencies, and education. There will be a big button to download my resume. I will add testimonials.

4. Work Section

This section will consist of examples of my work with titles and accordion modules that list credits and other details. The media will either be a video, image gallery, or embed. The image file I submitted has three works, but by the end of the bootcamp, I will assess any new projects for suitability for inclusion.

5. Contact Form

A basic contact form with inputs for name, email, subject, and message.

6. Footer

The footer will include my name, title, city, state, country, email, phone number, and linked social media icons.
