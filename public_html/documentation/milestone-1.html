Robyn Wagoner Personal Website Project

Milestone One

Website Purpose, Audience, and Goals

Purpose

Robyn's Personal Website aims to present her skills, experience, and projects in web development, serving as a comprehensive portfolio and professional hub. The site will highlight her expertise in both front-end and back-end technologies, demonstrating her ability to create efficient, user-friendly, and visually appealing websites. By showcasing her work and providing insights into her approach, Robyn's website seeks to establish her as a credible and talented web developer.

Audience

The primary audience for Robyn's Personal Website includes small to mid-sized non-profits looking for a part-time web developer, potential employers seeking skilled developers for full-time or contract positions, and fellow developers interested in her work and methodologies. Additionally, the site will cater to industry peers and professionals who may collaborate or refer her to new opportunities.

Goals

Robyn's quantifiable goal for her Personal Website is to attract at least three new freelance clients within the first six months of launching the site. She aims to secure contracts for website development, consultation, and ongoing maintenance, leveraging her expertise to support non-profit organizations in enhancing their digital presence. Additionally, she seeks to increase her professional network by engaging with industry peers and potential employers, measured by the number of inquiries and connections made through the website.
