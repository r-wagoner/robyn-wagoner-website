Robyn Wagoner's Personal Website Project

Milestone One

I. Webpage Purpose, Audience, and Goals

PAGE PURPOSE

Robyn intends to produce a webpage that can be migrated to her existing professional online portfolio and will present her skills, experience, and projects in web development. The page will highlight her expertise in both front-end and back-end technologies, demonstrating her ability to create secure, visually appealing and user-friendly websites, thereby establishing her as a credible and talented web developer who is currently accepting new clients.

PAGE AUDIENCE

The primary audience for Robyn's Personal Website includes small to mid-sized non-profits and cooperative businesses looking for a part-time web developer, potential employers seeking skilled developers for full-time or contract positions, and fellow developers interested in her work and methodologies. Additionally, the site will cater to industry peers and professionals who may collaborate or refer her to new opportunities.

PAGE GOALS

Robyn's quantifiable goal for her Personal Website is to attract at least three new freelance clients within the first six months of launching the site. She aims to secure contracts for website development, consultation, and ongoing maintenance, leveraging her expertise to support non-profit and cooperative organizations in enhancing their digital presence. Additionally, she seeks to increase her professional network by engaging with industry peers and potential employers, measured by the number of inquiries and connections made through the website.

II. User Persona: Janie E.

USER PROFESSION

Janie is the Executive Director of Willow River Equine Rescue, a mid-sized New Mexico 501(c)3 organization. Although 75% of their funding comes from grants and major donors, another 25% is raised from programs and merchandise that include animal husbandry classes and logo-ware, respectively. Janie’s non-profit is expanding its reach, and she recognizes the need for a professional and user-friendly website. Her current website is outdated, difficult to navigate, and does not effectively communicate their mission or accept online donations efficiently. Janie is looking for a reliable web developer who can redesign the website, make it easy to manage, and provide ongoing support and integration with social media and development campaigns.

USER BEHAVIORS AND ATTITUDES

Janie is proactive and organized, always seeking efficient solutions for her organization. She values clear communication and transparency from service providers. Janie has a basic understanding of web technologies but prefers to focus on her core responsibilities in fundraising, outreach, and program management. She often seeks out recommendations and reviews online before committing to a service provider.

USER PAIN POINTS

Janie has a limited budget for web development, and on-going security, and maintenance, as well as a lack of in-house technical expertise to handle serious website issues. Her current website’s poor user experience is deterring potential donors and volunteers. She has had Difficulty finding a communicative and trustworthy web developer. The User needs someone who is familiar with the situations of social-profit organizations. She needs someone who is highly responsive and genuinely cares about their success.

USER GOALS

Janie's goals involve finding a web developer who can create a visually appealing, easy-to-navigate website that effectively communicates their purpose and services. She wants to have a CMS that allows easy updates and management by the non-technical staff. She wants to ensure the website is optimized for SEO to increase visibility and improve user data security. This will require establishing a long-term relationship with a competent and reliable developer who can provide ongoing support and updates.
